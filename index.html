About Me

I'm Brad Cassity, a creative developer.

Pursuing a career in software development was a natural decision for me. The ability to develop custom solutions that solve problems for everyday people while introducing my own touch of creativity seeemed like an obvious destiny. My passion isn't to simply develop the easy stuff, but to tackle the hard problems that others avoid. Everyday is a new task that keeps my skills sharp while exposing me to new trends and technologies at a rapid rate.

It has always been inspiring working with like-minded developers who share similar ambitions as I and building something truly remarkable together. I have many hobbies and interests, but none of them stack up against how I feel from behind the wheel of a keyboard building the next big thing.
